History

Where

Millets were originated in southern Asia and is the
oldest of the cultivated millets. It is also known as Italian
or German Millet

Uses

Millets are used as breakfast, porridge dishes,
a replacement for rice alongside vegetable stir-
fries, and as a savory pilaf.

Types

Foxtail Millet, Little Millet, Barnyard Millet,
Kodo Millet,Brown Top Millet, Proso Millet, Finger Millet,
Multi Millet.

How

Helps in Weight loss

Millets help in weight loss. They contain Tryptophan, an amino acid that lowers appetite
and helps in managing weight. They are high in fibers and satiate hunger that prevents
overeting

Reduces Diabetes

Millets control insuin sensitivity for people suffering from diabetes and control the sugar

Rich in Fiber

Reduces High Blood Pressure

Millets contain magnesium that relaxes muscles in the arterial wall which in turn reduces
high BP. It also reduces the severity of Asthma and Migraine.
