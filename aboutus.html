About us

About Us

We Are Trusted By
People.

Our long drawn out mission to find and provide to you the world’s
best ingredients treats for cooking, eating and entertaining.


Our passion of Millet food and desire to reach them to the people of
India is inspiring us to work persistently to supply a full assortment of the
best & newest food products from around India, and in future, from
around the globe.


Khanasutra’s goal is, and has been since the beginning of our
business plans couple of years back, to discover, introduce and support
products that meet our and our customers uncompromising health &
quality standards.


Khanasutra presents Organic, Natural & Pure food products for
those who value quality & healthy living. Khanasutra’s vision
is to be a world class purveyor of healthy Millet Foods. High
quality natural and Pure food is what Khanasutra is striving
towards bringing towards bringing to every home.

Our Team

We are a group of working professionals as in doctors, nutritionist,
chartered accountants, chefs etc. We come together with a vision of
irradiating malnutrition by redesigning the dietary requirements of
individuals to ensure appropriate nutrition & long standing health.


Our research brought us to the wholesome qualities of millets which
are easily available, cost effective & healthy.

We plan to design our products on the basis of extensive research
on the biological significary of millets.

Our plan is to work at a grass root level unifying the producers
(farmers), with the consumers(end users) with health as a bonus.

We feel that a super food like millets have been under used &
hence the population at large is unable to get the maximum
benefit of this natures gift to humanity.

Our constant endeavor would be to increase awareness about this
super food & try to minimize the requirement of medicinal attributes to
help an individual in prime health.
